Important V2 notes

This is still not lab-grade foil power. It is a phone/body mechanical effort estimate. The best use is comparing runs: easy vs hard, different foils, cadence changes, and pump amplitude. The power calculation is intentionally filtered and capped to reduce fake 1500 W spikes from hand-shaking the phone.
