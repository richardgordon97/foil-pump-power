On iPhone, tap Enable Motion first. Then tap Start. Keep the phone tight to your torso in a vest, hydration pack, or waterproof pouch.

For water testing, use a waterproof case/pouch and keep the screen awake.

What the number means

The app estimates vertical rider force and vertical rider velocity, then calculates positive mechanical power. Formula: Power ≈ Force × Velocity. Force is estimated from rider mass and phone acceleration. Velocity is estimated by integrating acceleration, so the number will drift and should be treated as approximate.

V1 prototype — compare easy vs hard pumping, not absolute lab-grade watts.
